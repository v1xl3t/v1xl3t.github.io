What this is

A collective, not a company you disappear into.

HACKING PARADISE is a trial media-arts production and outsourcing studio and publishing collective. A group of multi-faceted creatives who send each other work, take on bigger clients together, and build a shared body of work and reputation, without giving up individual ownership or the freedom to work outside the studio.

Join us

Interested in becoming a member?

HACKING PARADISE is for people doing exceptional work across the arts and media. Membership is invitation and application based. When projects come in, members are offered paid roles at their own rates, and every offer is optional. A small 1.5 percent fee on each payout funds the studio (hosting, business costs, marketing, festival fees, a cushion, and a personal-project fund).
